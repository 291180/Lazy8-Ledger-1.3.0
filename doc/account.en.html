Account entry form
Here is where you enter your Accounts.   However, you might need to do some thing before you begin entering accounts here.  If the company you want to enter accounts on does not show up on the form, you must go to another form and enter the company number and name you are going to work with.  If this is your case, Go to the menu DataEntry->Company

The accounts you enter should be from some sort of standard accounting system .  For all examples here, we use the EU BAS 2000 account plan.  You are free to use any accounting system or make up your own.
 

Company

Choose here which company you want to work with.  Most users will have only one company to work with and in such a case then there should be no selection necessary and the company will come here automatically and in such a case this field will even be disabled so you wont be able to choose a company.  Please see SETUP  for more information on how to choose a default company and Company to see how to enter more companies.
 

Account number

This is an integer number that should come from some accepted accounting system.  The EU BAS 2000 system uses 4 digit numbers.  It is a good idea to give all your account numbers the same number of digits but there is no requirement for this program to work.  This number must be uniq for just this account you are entering for this particular company.

Account name

This can be any information you want.  Usually it appropriately describes the account.

Capital or debts

It is extreamly important that this check box is used correctly.  All accounts that are capital,loans, or inventory should have this box checked.  All accounts that are incomes or expendatures should not have this box checked.  This information is vital for doing the year end results.
